About Us

Welcome to Everest Coffee Shop, your cozy escape in the heart of the city. Inspired by the serene beauty and bold spirit of Mount Everest, we bring you a unique coffee experience that awakens your senses and warms your soul.

Founded in 2025, Everest Coffee Shop is passionate about quality, sustainability, and community. We carefully source our beans from ethical farms, roast them with precision, and brew each cup with love. Whether you're here for your morning espresso, a peaceful work session, or a relaxing chat with friends, you'll always find a welcoming atmosphere and the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee.

Come, explore our menu, enjoy our space, and let your coffee journey begin at Everest.
